Lineup

The Foreign Logic line-up

Foreign Logic brings together some of the best world talent in London: a classic rock formation and four voices locked into a sound built for live rooms.

Bass / Vocals

Dave Williamson

Originally from Aberdeen

Dave left Scotland in his early 20's to pursue a musical career in London.

Over the years he's been fortunate enough to have worked with many different bands, artists and writers from a wide range of genres, including Beverley Knight, Emma Bunton, Ronan Keating, Heatwave, Katrina and the Waves, The Crazy World of Arthur Brown, Diamond Head, Maggie Reilly, Russell Watson, Errol Brown, Junior Giscombe, Lisa Stansfield, Dina Carroll, Leo Sayer and Band Aid co-founder Midge Ure.

Watch Dave live with Midge Ure

Drums / Vocals

Mel Sarreal

Originally from Hong-Kong

Mel brings the pulse of Foreign Logic from behind the kit, shaping the band's rock sound with steady drive, sharp dynamics, and a feel for songs that need both power and space.

His drumming anchors the group live, giving the music its weight while leaving room for the guitars, keys, and vocals to move.

Watch Mel's drum solo

Guitar / Vocals

Mikko Malmivaara

Originally from Finland

Mikko adds the band's guitar edge, moving between riffs, textures, and melodic lines that give Foreign Logic its grit and atmosphere.

His playing brings a northern bite to the band's rock identity, balancing precision with the raw energy of a live performance.

Watch Mikko's disco composition

Keyboards / Vocals

Dani Colombo

Originally from Italy

Dani expands the band's sound from the keys, adding colour, hooks, and harmonic depth to Foreign Logic's rock arrangements.

His parts help connect the rhythm section and guitar work, giving the songs a wider sound without losing their direct, live-band feel.
